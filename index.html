    About Us

    We believe that differently-abled persons are like any other person, and have an equal vantage point when it comes to writing and recreating stories. Empowering disability by involving them in the media, films and entertainment was the motive with which our vision was born.

    We create opportunities for the disabled persons in social activities where they plan events and create content.

    Ideal-Able provides a voice to the otherwise silent. We provide support services to those who have disabilities, which media and film industries tend to not recognize or neglect. Working with national and international partners, we provide platforms for increased visibility within the media industry, creative engagement & awareness raising to inspire positive change.

    • Ideal-Able is a social enterprise that encourages disabled people to tell their own stories through the media.
    • Ideal-Able™ is a disability advocacy movement that champions the abilities of all people in the community, not just their disabilities.

    Partner with Us

    Becoming a partner means you can make a change that lasts. We believe in empowering others through our many programs to make positive impacts on the disable community. Join us in our mission to spread disability pride and awareness. Partner up with us and together we will develop an inclusive and sustainable society that embraces the value, equality and diversity of people with differently-abled. We offer three ways for your business to be a part of this exclusive program and reach out to the disability community.

    Enduring Partnerships

    we are looking for enduring partnerships with private and public sector organizations, where all members of the partnership can contribute and benefit social, environmental and economic outcomes.

    Segment Partnerships

    in these partnership sectors the private and public organizations select their desirable programs and partner with us.

    Specific partnerships

    These partnership sectors are very specific and precise in contrast to the others, this is where a private or a public organization or individual is helping a specific differently-abled individual till the very end achievement of their life goals.

    Programs

    The Focus On Ability Ethiopia

    We understand that it is difficult, frustrating and a weakness to all of them. The foundation of these platforms is primarily focused on the objective of enhancing access to justice to the disadvantaged section of society by providing them with their dream career, where it provides a comprehensive information system that provides assistance by linking industry professionals with their professions specialized areas. The platform is made of five categories in this section which includes media personnel, journalism, film industry and music industry.

    The Maleda Awards

    The Maleda awards is created to honor individuals and organizations whose work has contributed to the advancement of the disabled community. This is an important trophy that will be displayed proudly.This award provides inspiration to a person with an impairment or disability that might cause them to feel limited in some way. The Maleda Awards was once the “Focus On Ability Awards”, we changed the appellations and the team to separate the award and recognition program from a film festival.

    “Maleda” Training and consulting programs

    We offer training and consulting programs to people with disabilities and the agencies and organizations that serve them. Our mission is to provide individuals, businesses and agencies with real life solutions that enable Community living for people with disabilities. We strive towards this goal through our Working Solutions and Advocacy programs, working with individuals, teams and groups.

    “Differently- Abled” Digital Magazine and Media

    We provide weekly written magazines. Our initiative is not just to create ease and comfort of life for the physically challenged, but also to build awareness amongst everyone. The purpose of this platform is to provide weekly publications of the differently abled community and their activities/achievements.

    The Nova Employment Program

    The main objective of nova employment is to provide employment opportunities for disabled people and make them self-reliant. This platform gives the community an opportunity to get employed. It is our endeavor to provide proper facilities which will enable them to acquire, enhance and sustain new skills while working with us and thereby help them to get employed in corporate organizations on a permanent basis.

    Focus On Ability international Short Film Festival

    This part of our segment provides short film festivals on the disable community that creates awareness and education, the film festival is not just for people of the disable community and also for other filmmakers outside the disable community to generate awareness and the future ability of the community.

    Goals

    To empower individuals with physical, mental, or intellectual disabilities by giving them opportunity in the media and film. To enlighten and inspire more people about the abilities of disabled people and shed light on the hidden talents that go unnoticed.

    Inspiring people with disabilities to live their lives independently by becoming superstars in their own way. Helping people with disabilities change their lives and get first class jobs in a very short time.
